One-line: a compact icon-only button for toolbars, app bars and dense actions.

```jsx
<IconButton icon="search" label="검색" />
<IconButton icon="favorite" variant="tonal" fill />
<IconButton icon="more_vert" variant="standard" label="더보기" />
```

Variants: standard, filled, tonal, outlined. Always pass `label` for accessibility.
