One-line: the surface container for grouping content and actions.

```jsx
<Card variant="elevated" style={{padding:24}}>
  <h3 className="md-title-large">클라우드 전환</h3>
  <p className="md-body-medium">레거시 인프라를 안전하게 이전합니다.</p>
</Card>
```

Variants: elevated (default), filled, outlined. Add `interactive` for clickable cards. Supply your own inner padding.
